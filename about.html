About Me

Creative and detail-oriented, I am an aspiring web development specialist and current student at Joliet Junior College studying Web Design, Development and Social Media. I am experienced and knowledgeable in collaborative, cross-functional teams, inventory, and creating international relationships with overseas distributers.

In June 2020, at the height of COVID-19 and after being furloughed, I started to learn to code to occupy my time. I then discovered a passion and I decided to change my career and become a front-end developer. And in 2021 I started taking course part-time to continue learning and expand my network.

I am excited for the opportunity to join Founders and Coders and learn as well as contribute everything I can!
