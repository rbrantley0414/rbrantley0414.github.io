Branding - Crispy Waffle

Crispy Waffle is a casual, contemporary restaurant serving breakfast and lunch. The main goal was to rebrand the current identity and create a minimalistic and attractive brand identity inspired by 1950s American diners for this family-owned restaurants.

Branding includes: Logos, To-go Packaging, Brand Identity, Menus, and Apparel.
